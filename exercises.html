Exercise Tips for Indians

Make the most of your workout with these culturally relevant tips

Best Exercise Times

  • • Early morning (6-8 AM) - Fresh air, cooler temperature
  • • Evening (5-7 PM) - After work, before dinner
  • • Avoid midday heat (11 AM - 3 PM)

Weather Considerations

  • • Monsoon: Indoor exercises, yoga
  • • Summer: Early morning outdoor activities
  • • Winter: Take advantage of pleasant weather

Hydration Tips

  • • Drink water 30 mins before exercise
  • • Include electrolytes (nimbu paani, coconut water)
  • • Avoid exercising immediately after meals

Family Fitness

  • • Include family in evening walks
  • • Teach children traditional games
  • • Group yoga sessions on weekends
