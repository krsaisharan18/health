Indian Dietary Tips

Include More Dal

Dal is an excellent source of plant-based protein. Try different varieties like moong, masoor, and chana dal for variety.

Turmeric & Spices

Indian spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander have anti-inflammatory properties. Use them generously in your cooking.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water, especially in hot weather. Include coconut water, buttermilk, and fresh lime water for variety.

Meal Timing

Follow the traditional Indian meal pattern - light breakfast, heavy lunch, and early light dinner for better digestion.
